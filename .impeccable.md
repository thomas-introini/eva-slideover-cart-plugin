## Design Context

### Users
Primary users are WooCommerce store shoppers interacting with the slide-over cart during active purchase flow, with store admins as a secondary audience for configuration in WooCommerce settings.
The primary job to be done is a fast, low-friction path from add-to-cart to checkout while preserving enough clarity and control for quantity edits, removals, and subtotal awareness.

### Brand Personality
Brand personality: warm, modern, reliable.
Voice and tone should feel clear and reassuring, emphasizing calm confidence over hype or urgency.
Interface copy and interaction feedback should reduce uncertainty and support trust during purchase decisions.

### Aesthetic Direction
Keep and refine the existing warm palette direction (cream/orange/brown) as the default visual baseline.
Maintain the current clean, practical drawer UI with accessible affordances, subtle hierarchy, and restrained motion.
No explicit external references are required right now; avoid generic, overly playful, or aggressively sales-driven aesthetics that conflict with calm confidence.

### Design Principles
1. Prioritize checkout momentum: every interaction should reduce time and effort to reach checkout.
2. Keep users oriented: make cart state, price impact, and next actions instantly understandable.
3. Design for calm confidence: use warm, trustworthy visuals and concise, reassuring feedback.
4. Meet accessibility baseline by default: target WCAG 2.1 AA with strong keyboard/focus behavior, contrast, and touch targets.
5. Respect user comfort: include `prefers-reduced-motion` support for all new motion patterns.
